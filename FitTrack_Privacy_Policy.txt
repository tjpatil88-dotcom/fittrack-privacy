Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

1. Introduction
FitTrack Pro is a fitness tracking mobile application developed by Tejas. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. No Data Collection
FitTrack Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to any servers or third parties. The app works completely offline.

3. Local Storage Only
All workout data — including GPS routes, distance, speed, steps, calories, history, and statistics — is stored only on your device. No data is uploaded to the internet or shared with anyone.

4. Data Deletion
When you uninstall the app, all your workout data is automatically and permanently deleted from your device.

5. Permissions
The app requests the following permissions only for core functionality:
- Location — Used only during active workouts for GPS tracking.
- Physical Activity — Used for step counting.
- Notification — Used for voice announcements.

These permissions are required for the app to function. No data is sent off your device.
